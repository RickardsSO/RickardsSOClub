Registration Deadline - October 27

The competition will take place on our Scilympiad Page, So teams must be registered on our scilympiad page in order to participate.

Teams must be registered with their state science olympiad in order to participate in any invitationals.

How to

Each team will be $85 and title 1 schools can sign up for free. We don't want this fee to discourage anyone from participating so if your school is unable to pay, please email us at director@rsoi.org. Also, our district only allows us to recieve checks or purchase orders as payment, so we are unable to accept paypal.

  1. Please go over to our Scilympiad Page and log in.
  2. Go to the head coach dashboard and click register/update teams and add all the teams needed
  3. Click view/pay registration fees and send director@rsoi.org a picture of the check
  4. Follow the instructions on screen for sending the physical check and click pay by check to let us know it's on the way.

W9

We will provide a W9 if you email director@rsoi.org

Invoices

After clicking on pay by check, there will be a button to print invoice which will download a generated invoice.
